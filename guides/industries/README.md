# Industry-Specific Guides

Detailed accounting and financial management guides for 10 different business types. Each guide covers:
- Industry-specific challenges
- Critical metrics to track
- Unique deductions
- Common mistakes
- Financial management timeline
- When to hire help

---

## 10 Industry Guides

### 1. [E-Commerce & Online Retail](./e-commerce.md)
**Key Challenges**: Multi-channel revenue tracking, cost of goods sold, marketplace fees, inventory management, returns & refunds

**Critical Metrics**: Profit margin by product, customer acquisition cost, customer lifetime value, return rate, inventory turnover

**Unique Focus**: Marketplace fee analysis, inventory valuation, customer retention vs. acquisition cost

**Perfect For**: Amazon sellers, Etsy shop owners, Shopify stores, online retailers

---

### 2. [SaaS & Tech Startups](./saas-tech.md)
**Key Challenges**: Revenue recognition (deferred revenue), churn tracking, CAC payback period, venture capital metrics

**Critical Metrics**: Monthly recurring revenue (MRR), customer acquisition cost, customer lifetime value, churn rate, payback period

**Unique Focus**: Burn rate management, cap table tracking, venture capital financials, subscription economics

**Perfect For**: Software companies, SaaS businesses, tech startups, app developers

---

### 3. [Food & Beverage](./food-beverage.md)
**Key Challenges**: Inventory management (perishable), tip tracking, daily cash reconciliation, complex labor costs

**Critical Metrics**: Food cost percentage (target: 28-35%), labor cost percentage (target: 28-35%), prime cost (target: 60-65%), menu item profitability

**Unique Focus**: Inventory shrinkage, waste tracking, labor efficiency, menu engineering

**Perfect For**: Restaurants, cafes, fast casual, catering, food trucks

---

### 4. [Nonprofits](./nonprofits.md)
**Key Challenges**: Fund accounting (restricted vs. unrestricted), Form 990 compliance, grant management, donor reporting

**Critical Metrics**: Program ratio (target: 75%+ to programs), fundraising efficiency (target: 10-15%), donor retention, grant success rate

**Unique Focus**: Compliance (501(c)(3) maintenance), grant tracking, in-kind donations, Form 990 filing

**Perfect For**: Charities, nonprofits, faith organizations, educational nonprofits, community services

---

### 5. [Manufacturing & Export](./manufacturing-export.md)
**Key Challenges**: Multiple inventory levels (raw materials, WIP, finished goods), cost allocation, equipment depreciation, multi-currency management

**Critical Metrics**: Gross margin, operating margin, inventory turnover, production efficiency, lead time

**Unique Focus**: COGS calculation complexity, quality control costs, export compliance, tariff optimization

**Perfect For**: Manufacturers, exporters, distributors, supply chain businesses

---

### 6. [Healthcare & Medical Practices](./healthcare-medical.md)
**Key Challenges**: Insurance billing complexity, patient receivables, claims denials, HIPAA compliance, deferred revenue

**Critical Metrics**: Claims denial rate (target: <5%), days sales outstanding, patient aging report, operating margin (target: 15-25%)

**Unique Focus**: Insurance verification, claims follow-up/appeals, patient collection, malpractice insurance deductions

**Perfect For**: Medical practices, dental offices, therapy practices, healthcare clinics

---

### 7. [Freelance & Consulting Services](./freelance-consulting.md)
**Key Challenges**: Unbilled work tracking, client aging/receivables, rate pricing strategy, utilization rate optimization

**Critical Metrics**: Effective hourly rate, billable rate, utilization rate (target: 70-80%), revenue per client, days sales outstanding

**Unique Focus**: Project profitability, client concentration risk, rate increases, scaling strategy

**Perfect For**: Consultants, freelancers, coaches, experts, service providers

---

### 8. [Salons & Beauty Services](./salons-beauty.md)
**Key Challenges**: Commission payouts (stylist/service provider splits), prepaid packages, tip accounting, retail inventory vs. service revenue

**Critical Metrics**: Average ticket size, service mix, stylist productivity, inventory turnover, commission expense as % of revenue

**Unique Focus**: Commission accuracy, prepaid package liability, product vs. service margin analysis, staff retention

**Perfect For**: Hair salons, beauty salons, spas, nail salons, wellness centers

---

### 9. [Coaching & Training](./coaching-training.md)
**Key Challenges**: Deferred revenue (prepaid programs), cancellations/refunds, multiple revenue streams, contractor/affiliate payments

**Critical Metrics**: Conversion rate, cost per acquisition, client lifetime value, course completion rate, churn rate

**Unique Focus**: Program profitability by type, scaling from 1-on-1 to group, subscription economics

**Perfect For**: Life coaches, business coaches, trainers, course creators, membership builders

---

### 10. [Creative Agencies](./creative-agencies.md)
**Key Challenges**: Time tracking & allocation accuracy, scope creep, project profitability, contractor cost management

**Critical Metrics**: Billable utilization rate (target: 70-80%), effective hourly rate, project margin, client profitability, scope adherence

**Unique Focus**: Scope management, project estimation accuracy, client profitability analysis, contractor cost tracking

**Perfect For**: Design agencies, marketing agencies, web development, digital agencies, advertising agencies

## Common Themes Across All Industries

### 1. Know Your Numbers
Every industry benefits from weekly tracking, monthly analysis, quarterly strategy review.

### 2. Pricing Discipline
Most entrepreneurs underprice. Raising rates by 10-20% usually increases profit 40-50%.

### 3. Client/Customer Profitability
Some are highly profitable, others drain time/energy. Identify and address the unprofitable.

### 4. Proper Accounting Foundation
Bookkeeping seems tedious but saves thousands in taxes and prevents costly mistakes.

### 5. When to Hire Help
Hiring a bookkeeper at $200-500/month usually pays for itself 3-5x through tax savings and efficiency.
