Everything you need to know before getting started with a professional bookkeeper.

Budget does not mean low quality. It means accessible, transparent pricing aligned with early-stage and growing businesses. Our bookkeepers offer fair rates with clear breakdowns of all costs involved. We believe every woman entrepreneur deserves professional financial support regardless of her current revenue level.
Our platform is built on comfort and trust. Many women entrepreneurs prefer collaborative communication styles and shared understanding of life stages. A female-only network reduces friction, encourages clarity, and increases psychological safety in financial discussions. This is empowerment through alignment, not exclusion.
Bookkeeping costs vary by business stage: Starter plans range from $0-$150/month for new businesses, Growth plans from $150-$400/month for established businesses, Scaling plans from $400-$1,000/month for rapidly growing companies, and Complex plans at $1,000+ for multi-entity operations. Every provider on our platform clearly displays their rates and what is included.
Bookkeeping is the day-to-day recording of financial transactions including income, expenses, invoices, and bank reconciliation. Tax preparation is the annual process of filing tax returns using bookkeeping records. Many bookkeepers offer both services, but they are distinct specializations. Good bookkeeping makes tax preparation dramatically easier and cheaper.
